AGRICULTURE PRODUCT

Agricultural product of agricultural origin means any product or commodity raw or processed, that is marketed for human consumption excluding water, salt and additives or animal feed.

crops fall into six categories:

  • food crops
  • fiber crops
  • ornamental crops
  • industrial crops
  • feed crops
  • oil crops

What are the important of agriculture?

  • supporting livelihoods through food
  • habitat
  • providing raw materials for food and other products
  • building strong economies through trade
